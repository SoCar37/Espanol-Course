## Learning outcomes

By completing A1, learners will be able to:
- Greet people and introduce themselves
- Ask and answer questions about personal details
- Interact in a simple way when the other person speaks slowly and clearly
- Use numbers, tell the time, and discuss dates
- Describe their immediate environment and basic everyday needs
