# Weather & Seasons

Talking about the weather is a universal conversation starter. In Mexican Spanish, weather expressions use the verb **hacer** (to make/do) in a unique way — you'll learn this pattern and the vocabulary to describe any type of weather.

## Weather Expressions with Hacer

In Spanish, many weather expressions use **hace** (third person singular of hacer):

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| Hace calor. | It's hot. |
| Hace frío. | It's cold. |
| Hace viento. | It's windy. |
| Hace sol. | It's sunny. |
| Hace buen tiempo. | The weather is nice. |
| Hace mal tiempo. | The weather is bad. |

## Other Weather Expressions

Some weather conditions use different verbs:

| Spanish | English | Verb used |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Está lloviendo. | It's raining. | estar + gerund |
| Está nevando. | It's snowing. | estar + gerund |
| Está nublado. | It's cloudy. | estar |
| Hay niebla. | There's fog. | hay |
| Hay tormenta. | There's a storm. | hay |

## The Seasons

| Spanish | English | Pronunciation |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| la primavera | spring | pree-mah-BEH-rah |
| el verano | summer | beh-RAH-noh |
| el otoño | autumn / fall | oh-TOH-nyoh |
| el invierno | winter | een-BYEHR-noh |

> En México, **el verano** es la temporada de lluvias. *(In Mexico, summer is the rainy season.)*

## Asking About the Weather

> — ¿Qué tiempo hace hoy? *(What's the weather like today?)*
> — Hace mucho calor. *(It's very hot.)*

> — ¿Va a llover? *(Is it going to rain?)*
> — Sí, está muy nublado. *(Yes, it's very cloudy.)*

## Temperature

> — ¿A cuántos grados está? *(What's the temperature?)*
> — Está a treinta grados. *(It's thirty degrees.)*

Mexico uses **Celsius** (grados centígrados).

## 🇲🇽 Cultural Note

Mexico has enormous climate diversity. Mexico City sits at 2,240 meters altitude and has a mild, temperate climate year-round — locals say it has "eternal spring." Coastal areas like Cancún and Acapulco are hot and humid. Northern states like Chihuahua get cold winters and hot summers. The rainy season (*temporada de lluvias*) runs from June to October across most of the country — afternoon showers are almost daily in summer.
